Background

  1. The Ngā Puanga Pūtaiao Fellowships support Māori and Pacific Peoples to establish careers in Science, Technology, Engineering and mathematics (STEM) research and create a support network of Māori and Pacific STEM researchers.  This will enable Māori and Pacific early- to mid-career STEM researchers to enter into and progress through the Research, Science and Innovation (RSI) workforce and provide the researchers with support to flourish within the RSI system.
  2. On behalf of MBIE, the Society is responsible for awarding and managing funding for Ngā Puanga Pūtaiao Fellowships.
  3. The Society has awarded a Fellowship to {{ c.key_person.full_name_with_title }} (Key Contact Person) and, as part of it, will provide funding for the Key Contact Person to undertake a Programme.  The Host Organisation has agreed to host and support the Key Contact Person in relation to that Programme.
  4. This Agreement records the arrangements reached between the Society and the Host Organisation for the provision of Ngā Puanga Pūtaiao Fellowship funding for the research undertaken by the Key Contact Person and Other Key Personnel.
